# Vision & OKRs

## Vision
Deliver an open-source, AI-augmented email platform that organizations can self-host with a Python-first backend, ensuring privacy, extensibility, and rapid deployment.

## FY2025 Objectives & Key Results

### Objective 1 — Ship core email functionality with Python stack
- **KR1**: Launch pilot-ready build with ≥3 internal teams by Feb 2026.
- **KR2**: Deliver complete feature set across authentication, inbox, compose.
- **KR3**: Document all APIs with OpenAPI and publish Docusaurus site.

### Objective 2 — Ensure reliability & compliance
- **KR1**: Meet 99.9% availability SLO measured over rolling 30 days.
- **KR2**: Pass internal security review with no critical findings.
- **KR3**: Implement GDPR export/delete within first two releases.

### Objective 3 — Drive AI adoption responsibly
- **KR1**: ≥60% of users view AI summaries weekly.
- **KR2**: Secure at least two AI providers integrated via plugin architecture.
- **KR3**: Collect qualitative feedback from ≥10 users per quarter.

> Review OKRs at sprint retro and adjust quarterly.
