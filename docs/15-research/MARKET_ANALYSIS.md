# Market Analysis

## Competitors
- **HEY by Basecamp** — Premium email experience, closed-source.
- **Superhuman** — Fast premium client, no self-host option.
- **Nylas** — API-first, developer platform (not full client).
- **ProtonMail** — Privacy-focused, self-hosted option available.

## Differentiators
- Python-first backend lowers barrier for teams invested in FastAPI.
- Self-host friendly with Mongo (document storage suits email payloads).
- Extensible AI agent framework (plug-in providers).
- Compliance-ready deployment (private clouds, data residency).

## Target segments
- Mid-sized enterprises wanting AI email internally.
- Tech companies with Python expertise.
- Privacy-first organizations needing on-prem option.

## Go-to-market notes
- Start with internal adoption (dogfooding), gather testimonials.
- Offer managed hosting service for pilot customers.
- Highlight Python-first backend and self-hosting capabilities.

> Update quarterly with market feedback.
